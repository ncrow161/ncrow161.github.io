About Me

This is what I do

Hey! Thanks for checking out my website!

My name is Natalie Crowe. I'm a high school senior about to start the next terrifying chapter of my life: college. Starting this fall, 2023, I'll be an adult--which does not seem right considering that Goldfish is still my favorite snack and I still like reading Warriors. But whatever, life happens.

If you check out the rest of my site, you'll probably learn some stuff about me: 
First and foremost, I'm a daughter of the King. I believe that Christ Jesus died for my sins and was risen again by God, and that His sacrifice is enough to wash my sins away. I know where I'm going when I die, and I'd love to talk to you about it!
Second, I love words. Writing them, reading them, speaking them (I talk a lot)--I love it all. I want to write things that will change the world.
Third, I love to crochet. I love making pretty things out of scraps of yarn. It reminds me how God shapes us to be amazing, creative beings out of nothing.

Thanks again for checking out my blog! I hope you're encouraged by what you read.
